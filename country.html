Country Posters

Our task for this project was to create 3 posters, inspired on 3 different cities. Our only criteria, was the colours needed to relate to the city chosen.

New york

My first poster was inspired by New York. I chose yellow and black for this design, as when i think of New York, i think of yellow taxi's. Which also inspired the design. Using the work 'taxi' in a typography form to create a yellow taxi.
